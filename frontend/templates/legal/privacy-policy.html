Privacy policy.

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information we collect

To provide the Gor://a Builder service, coordinate your AI instances, and host your WebContainers, we collect and store data in the following categories:

  • Account data: email address, display name, and active subscription plan details.
  • Project content: source code (HTML/CSS/JS), virtual file structures, prompt chat histories, and AI-generated project snapshots (Base64).
  • Usage metrics: token consumption logs, API Gateway usage, and daily credit "spin" metrics.
  • Infrastructure data: active project subdomains and connected GitHub repository URLs.

2. Third-party integrations & tokens

Gor://a Builder acts as a bridge between your ideas and your deployment environment. To automate your workflow, we securely store the following sensitive third-party credentials:

  • GitHub access tokens: used exclusively to push generated boilerplate and WebContainer code directly to your repositories.
  • Figma access tokens: used via OAuth to read structural design files and nodes (JSON) to convert visual layouts into functional code.

Security guarantee: Your access tokens are strictly isolated. They are never exposed to the public internet, never written to your generated code's .env files, and are used exclusively by our backend to execute the actions you request.

3. How your data is used

We do not sell your project ideas or code. Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate, compile, and host your web applications via WebContainers.
  • Facilitate high-speed AI generation via our internal Proxy Gateway.
  • Authenticate your gb_live_ key against external LLM providers (e.g. OpenRouter, Fireworks).

4. Data retention & deletion

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard PostgreSQL (Supabase) encryption. You retain full ownership of your code. You may request the total deletion of your account, projects, and all associated third-party tokens at any time through your dashboard settings.
